Welcome to Apex Legends™ — the fast-paced, squad-based battle royale that brings skill, strategy, and hero shooter mechanics to the Outlands.

About the Game

Apex Legends™ is a free-to-play first-person shooter developed by Respawn Entertainment. Set in the Titanfall universe, it blends the thrill of battle royale with the depth of a hero shooter. Each Legend offers unique tactical, passive, and ultimate abilities, fostering strategic gameplay and dynamic team compositions.

The game features rotating maps, limited-time events, ranked modes, and a continuously evolving story delivered through seasons.

Best Landing Spots (Season 21)

Thunderdome – Kings Canyon

A highly contested POI with top-tier loot and fast access to The Cage and Skulltown. Good for high-kill games.

Climatizer – World’s Edge

Reliable high-tier loot, great zipline access, and quick rotations to Fragment East or Epicenter.

Breaker Wharf – Storm Point

Newer area with great loot, relatively low drop heat, and escape options via gravity cannon.

Hammond Labs – Olympus

Central location, clean layout, and good loot flow. Easily rotate to Estates or Power Grid.

Top Weapons (S21 Meta)

R-301 Carbine

Still the go-to for consistent mid-range accuracy and control. Works well with 2x or 3x scopes.

Nemesis Burst AR

A high-powered burst rifle that charges up over time. Dominant in close-to-mid engagements.

Peacekeeper

High burst damage shotgun with precision choke. Perfect for aggressive close-range plays.

Volt SMG

Reliable, low-recoil energy SMG. Excellent time-to-kill and mobility for close-range specialists.

Supported Platforms & Launchers

  • Steam: Fully supported. Syncs with your EA account. Features achievements, cloud saves, and seasonal events.
  • EA App (formerly Origin): Official EA launcher for PC users. Fully integrated with all EA titles.
  • Consoles: Available on PlayStation 4, PlayStation 5, Xbox One, Xbox Series X|S, and Nintendo Switch.
  • Crossplay: Supported across all platforms. Cross-progression is planned but not fully available as of Season 21.
